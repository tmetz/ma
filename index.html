About the Three-Bucket Strategy

  • Tax-Deferred Bucket: Traditional 401(k)s, Traditional IRAs - Tax deduction now, pay taxes in retirement
  • Tax-Free Bucket: Roth 401(k)s, Roth IRAs - Pay taxes now, tax-free growth and withdrawals
  • Taxable Bucket: Regular brokerage accounts, savings accounts - No tax advantages, but maximum flexibility
